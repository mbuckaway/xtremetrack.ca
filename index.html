You've head of Six Day Racing. You've seen local track racing. The Forest City Velodrome Challenge XTremeTrack 1001 event features racing from some of the best and brightest talent from Canada, the USA and the World coming together to compete for $16,000 in cash prizes.

Racing will feature youth/jr/adults men and women on Saturday afternoon with the Pro 1000 lap challenge Saturday evening. On Sunday, the Pro Women will take to the track in the afternoon to compete for their own 1001 lap challenge.

Specators welcome! Entry is $15 for the entire weekend! Food and beverages will be on site!

Competitors

14 Pro Men and 14 Pro women confirmed for the event!

Cash Prizes!

$16,000 in cash prizes and primes are up for grabs! $3000 for the 1001 lap challenge main event for men AND women. Equal prizing for men and women!

Location

Located about 1.5hrs from Toronto and about 2.5hrs from Detroit, the Forest City Velodrome is located off the Wellington St exit in London, ON
